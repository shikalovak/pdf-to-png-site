How to convert PDF to PNG in Chrome

  1. Install the “PDF to PNG” Chrome extension.
  2. Pin it to your toolbar for quick access.
  3. Click the extension icon and choose a PDF file.
  4. The extension converts each page locally and shows you a PNG preview.
  5. Download the pages you need as PNG images.

Why use a PDF to PNG extension instead of a website?

  • Privacy first. Your PDF files never leave your device – there are no uploads to third-party servers.
  • Works offline. Once installed, the extension works even without an internet connection.
  • Fast previews. Convert and preview pages directly in your browser popup.
  • Always at hand. No need to search for a converter website – the extension lives in your Chrome toolbar.

FAQ

Is the extension really free?
Yes. You can install and use it for free. If you like it, you can support the project with a small donation.
Are my documents uploaded anywhere?
No. All conversion happens directly in your browser using local processing. Your PDF files never leave your computer.
Can I use it without an internet connection?
Yes. After installation from the Chrome Web Store, the extension can convert PDFs to PNGs even when you are offline.
