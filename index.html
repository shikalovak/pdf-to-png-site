How to convert PDF to PNG in Chrome

This PDF to PNG converter is a lightweight browser extension. It helps you quickly export PDF pages as PNG images without uploading files to external servers. Everything runs directly in your browser using local processing.

To convert a PDF to PNG images:

  • Install the PDF to PNG Chrome extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  • Open a PDF file in your browser or select a PDF from your computer.
  • Click the extension icon and choose which pages you want to convert.
  • Download the generated PNG images to your device.

This tool is ideal if you need to extract slides, screenshots, or separate pages from a PDF document and save them as PNG files.

Why use a PDF to PNG extension

Compared to online PDF to PNG services, a browser extension gives you several advantages:

  • No need to upload sensitive documents to third-party servers.
  • Fast conversion, because everything happens locally in your browser.
  • Works even when you are offline or have a slow internet connection.
  • Simple interface focused only on converting PDF to PNG, without ads or distractions.

If you regularly work with PDFs from email, cloud storage, or internal systems, having a one-click PDF to PNG converter in Chrome can save a lot of time.

FAQ: PDF to PNG converter

Is this PDF to PNG converter free?

Yes, the Chrome extension is free to install and use. You can convert as many PDF files to PNG as you need without limits.

Do my PDF files leave my computer?

No, your PDF files are processed directly in your browser. The extension does not upload your documents to remote servers.

What image quality do I get?

The extension converts each PDF page to a high-resolution PNG image, suitable for presentations, printing, or sharing.

Can I choose specific pages to convert?

Yes, you can select individual pages or convert the entire PDF to a set of PNG files.

Which browsers are supported?

The extension is designed for Google Chrome. It may also work in Chromium-based browsers that support Chrome extensions.
